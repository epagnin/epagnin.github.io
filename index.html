Hi! 👋

I am an Assistant Professor (Forskarassistent - Tenure-Track) at the Computer Science and Engineering department at Chalmers University (Göteborg, Sweden), where I am head of the Cryptoteam and part of the Security & Privacy Lab.

I like research that addresses open challenges and makes feasible what was not feasible before.
