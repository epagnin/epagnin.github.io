Mentoring videos by the IACR community

The International Association for Cryptologic Research (IACR) launched a initiative to provide advice for researchers in early stages (students and junior faculty) and women researchers in the area of cryptography. In this serie of videos you can listen to the answers a pool of community members gives to a wide range of questions, most of which you will stumble upon at a certain point in your academic carier.

Some videos are quite long, use the links given in the video description to jump directly to the advice of researchers that resonate more with you.
